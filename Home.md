## How to study

1. Open a **MOC** and pick a topic.
2. Follow wikilinks / backlinks between related notes.
3. Search by tag when you remember the idea, not the title (e.g. `tag:#idempotency`, `tag:#aws`, `tag:#lateral-join`).
4. For Problems: read the **scenario** first, then the **companion**.
5. For AWS: guide → quiz → hints, then Practice / Reviews.
